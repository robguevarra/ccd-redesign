After graduating top in his High School class as Valedictorian, he was selected as one of the few in the nation to attend the Accelerated Dental Advanced Placement Tract at USC. He then became the youngest doctor in his class to earn the Doctor of Dental Surgery degree – in fact one of the youngest in USC Dental School history. Dr. Hsu went on to specialize in the medical and dental field, earning his Master of Science in Orofacial Pain, Oral Medicine and Sleep Disorders at USC.
