#### [About](https://dentisthsu.com/doctors/dr-angela-huang/\#1438945227793-d0060e50-3f9b)

Dr. Angela Huang is a general dentist who has a passion for cosmetic and restorative dentistry. She earned her BS in Biological Science at the University of California, Irvine (UCI). In 2008 she graduated from the Temple University School of Dentistry, earning her Doctorate in Dental Medicine. As a dentist, she spent time with the Student Outreach Program at Temple University School of Dentistry, where she performed dental work on underprivileged patients at various designated dental group facilities. Dr. Angela has earned certification in Tissue Management for Implants and Advanced Dental Implant & Reconstructive Surgery Training Program. During her free time she enjoys baking, running, and spending time with her family.
