## Financing

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| When it comes to paying for dental treatment, we try and work with all our patients. We always make every effort to provide an accurate estimate of what the patient’s dental insurance will cover. Dental procedures are usually divided up, so that payments can be made. We accept all major forms of credit cards and checks. If for some reason patients still have a hard time making their payments, we offer extended payment plans. Because we are not a lending agency, we go through a third party for financing: Care Credit. For those patients who qualify, Care Credit can offer no interest for 3 to 12 months. There are no fees involved with these plans and no interest as long as the balance is paid in full within the plan period of 3, 6, or 12 months. Money does not grow on trees, so how is Care Credit able to do so without any fees? <br>During the period of zero percent financing, our office pays the fees directly to the third party… because we believe that when it comes to our patient’s dental health care, financing fees should not be an obstacle. | ![Dental Financing](http://2017.dentisthsu.com/images/MoneyTreeFinal.jpg) |
