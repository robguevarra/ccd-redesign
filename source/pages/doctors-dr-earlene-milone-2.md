#### [About](https://dentisthsu.com/doctors/dr-earlene-milone-2/\#1438945227793-d0060e50-3f9b)

Dental Hygiene Specialist Dr. Amandeep Singh earned his Bachelor of Science in Biology at University of California, Irvine. He then continued his education at the University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine, where he earned his Doctorate of Dental Medicine. His accomplishments include Assistant Instructor to first year dental students on periodontal treatment, Deans Honor Roll, and Secretary and Treasurer of the Medical Health Science Society. Dr. Singh has been specializing in performing dental hygiene procedures since 2006. He is known by many of his patients for providing professional, yet gentle periodontal dental procedures.
